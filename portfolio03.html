Practicing HTML/CSS to Build a Tribute Page to My Childhood Idol

This Derrick Rose tribute page is a responsive and accessible single-page web project showcasing my proficiency in HTML5 and CSS3. Created as part of the freeCodeCamp certification, this tribute page highlights the career milestones of Derrick Rose, the youngest MVP in NBA history. The design features a prominent headline, an engaging image section with captions, a detailed timeline of his achievements, and an inspiring quote, all structured with semantic HTML. Through clean, visually appealing layouts and effective use of hyperlinks, this project demonstrates my ability to design informative, user-friendly, and standards-compliant web pages.
